## ⚡ Start with UniFi

UniFi is a **global stablecoin payment platform** that enables:

- 🚫⛽ Gasless transactions
- 🌍 Multi-chain payments
- ⚡ Instant & low-cost transfers
- 🔐 Privacy-first design
- 💸 Bulk & scheduled payments

<Note>    
Built for real-world payments — from freelancers to enterprises.
</Note>
